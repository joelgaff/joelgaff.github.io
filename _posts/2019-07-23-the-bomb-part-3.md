We’re finally looking well beyond the explosion. Our 13-week old little _bomb_ has the most lovely smile, quiet coos, and big, beautiful eyes. She’s sleeping a stretch of at least eight hours a night, and goes 12 hours between feedings at night.

Her five week early arrival is now almost an afterthought, and she’s wooing us with her everyday achievements.
